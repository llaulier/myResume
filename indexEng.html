Web developer Student JavaScript - React - Node.js

For a long time, I struggled with figuring out what I wanted to do when I grew up. After studying literature, I was pushed towards becoming a teacher, but I knew that teaching wasn't my calling.

My mind was always racing with ideas and it is hard to follow me.

For ten years, I lived several lives.

I worked in research for big corporations such as Deloitte and Taj, and delving into legal and economic documentation. I also became a communications officer, but the political nature of the job didn't align with my interests.

Instead, I was drawn to the technical aspects of the field, especially when it came to web development.

I warned you, I am a slasher. But a slasher passionate about tech.

In 2020, I decided to take the plunge and retrain as a web developer. Despite the challenges brought on by the COVID pandemic, I persevered, determined to learn as much as I could. I even obtained a diploma in web development, which gave me the confidence to pursue new opportunities.

One such opportunity came in the form of an internship at a dynamic startup called Algar. They were looking for someone who was skilled in Integromat, a powerful automation tool that I was eager to learn. I joined the team and was immediately blown away by the possibilities of Nocode. It was a game-changer that opened up new doors for me in the tech world.
